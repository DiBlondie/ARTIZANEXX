THE HIDDEN WONDERS OF ART

The Myopic Mystic arts is a combination of an abstract mindset and divine procreation of thoughts and words. This genre of art was borne out of the exceptional taste in realistic abstract, while we breathe into the art, the art breathe into us back, hereby establishing a rare kind of divine connection between human and art.

The Peeper

This piece of art centers round the distinct importance of the eyes, the struggle it goes through to oversee all physical materials. It also emphasizes on the beauty of this creation and its exceptionality because without the eyes, the other parts of the body could be compared to a half instead of a whole.

The Psyche

A man's greatest and deadliest asset is his MIND. His reasoning is thrown into an abyss of confusion as soon as he finds himself in an unsolvable quagmire. But who can he run to? Being a man, he's always expected to be strong and firm. But look at him, is there any more words to describe his feelings other than disassembled, lost or even confused? I doubt.

The helpless colleen

The abused, the manhandled or rather the oppressed have nothing to say other than hide beneath their skin and curse under their breaths. The world they say is a cruel place to stay, while the influenced get away with the most grievous offences, the less privilegd gets the penalty of the most grievous offences when they make simple mistakes. The world will be a better place to stay. But how long do we hope for? Don't know? Me too.

SYNOPSIS

The generality of this art emphasizes on the extreme bonding of human nature and spirituality. Without art, as a expression of vulnerability, human nature is seen as a quadriplegic tree without no limbs at all. The existence of this art brings life to death, and response to numbness.
