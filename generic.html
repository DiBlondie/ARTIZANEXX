BURNING DESIRE

The burning desire is an art borne out of the existence of its nature known as pyrography. Art is meant to be expressed in any language but interpreting it requires a great deal of knowledge and understanding. This piece of art work illuminates on the bond between fire and earth, herby making one incomplete without the other.

This piece of art as seen is clearly detailed image of Princess Adaeze of the Great Igbo Ukwu Kingdom. While this piece of art illuminates the beauty and perfection of the feminine gender, it also talks about the importance of royalty and symbolises wealth, creation and superiority.
